Shipping Policy

When you shop online, here's what you need to do to get free shipping:

1. Make sure you have at least $25 worth of eligible items in your Shopping Cart. Look for items marked with "FREE Shipping" on their product page.

2. Go to checkout when you're ready.

3. Enter a delivery address.

4. Choose FREE Shipping as your shipping option.
